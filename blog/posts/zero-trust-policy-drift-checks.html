Zero Trust Policy Drift Checks in a Home Lab

Zero Trust · Published Mar 12, 2026 · 7 min read

Policy drift is one of the easiest ways to lose Zero Trust posture without noticing. In a home lab, drift usually comes from emergency allow rules and one-off troubleshooting changes that never get rolled back.

Simple fix: track every temporary rule with an expiry window, run weekly rule-diff checks, and validate east-west traffic paths against your intended matrix.
