About

Home lab builds. Networking experiments. Systems engineering notes.

BigHomieCed is a hands-on engineering sandbox documenting how infrastructure is designed, tested, broken, rebuilt, and hardened in a controlled lab environment.
This site focuses on practical work across:
- Home lab architecture and virtualization
- Network segmentation and VLAN design
- Firewall architecture, rule design, and traffic control
- Layer 2 and Layer 3 routing labs and packet flow analysis
- Infrastructure automation with scripts, repeatable tasks, and operational tooling
- Hardening patterns, validation steps, and “what went wrong” build logs
What you will find here:

Build Logs (Lab Notes). Step-by-step writeups of lab work, including configuration decisions, validation checks, and failure analysis when a design breaks.

Project Pages. Longer-form documentation for complete builds: lab topology, routing paths, firewall zones, service isolation, and operational outcomes.

Diagrams and Topology. Network diagrams and infrastructure maps showing segmentation, trust boundaries, and packet flow through the lab.

Repeatable Engineering. The goal is not “look what I did once,” but repeatable patterns: consistent setups, predictable rollback plans, and documented troubleshooting.
Design Principles:

Real systems, real constraints. Lab content is written with production thinking: least privilege, explicit policies, observability, and rollback plans.

Clarity over hype. Pages prioritize concrete outcomes: configurations, diagrams, validations, and the reasoning behind decisions.

Document what fails. Failures are part of the work. Build logs include what broke, why it broke, and how the fix changed the design.

Sustainable support. Advertising may appear to support hosting and development. Core reading and navigation are not intended to be paywalled.
BigHomieCed is built for engineers, builders, and technically curious people who want practical lab documentation—networking, firewalls, routing, automation, and systems design—without the noise of a social platform.
